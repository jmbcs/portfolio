About

My name is Júlio Silva and I'm a Software Engineer from Viseu, Portugal. With a background in Electronics and Telecommunications, I transitioned into software development with a focus on building efficient backend systems.

My journey started during my thesis at the University of Aveiro — an image retrieval system that associated images with text — where I discovered the power and flexibility of Python. Since then, I've worked in both hybrid and remote environments, building microservices, REST APIs, and scalable backend architecture using Django, FastAPI, Golang, and PostgreSQL.

What drives me is the thrill of problem-solving. I enjoy facing a challenge I don't yet know how to solve, and piecing things together until the solution clicks. Professionally, I aim to grow into a technical leadership role while staying hands-on with code.
